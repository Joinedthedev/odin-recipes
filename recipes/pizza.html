Pizza Recipe

Pizza is an italian delicacy consisting of three main ingredients: bread, tomato sauce, and cheese. Yup that's it simple, yet so delicious. ALthough pizza may have originated in Italy, many countries across the world have adopted this food and made various innovations making pizza the ultimate international dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pizza crust or Italian bread shell
  • 3 plum tomato (blank)s plum tomatoes, thinly sliced
  • ⅓ cup BUITONI® Refrigerated Pesto with Basil
  • 1½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper

Directions

Step 1

Preheat oven to 450 F. Place pizza crust on baking sheet.

Step 2

Spread pesto over pizza crust. Arrange tomatoes over pesto. Sprinkle with cheese and crushed red pepper.

Step 3

Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown. Cut into wedges.
